14 years of tradition

Fitness club "Versus" is formed 2006. year.At the beginning of the work, club worked as a section za aerobic body building, but from 20. june 2008. year as independent sporty club.Our team every year wins awards that prove we are at the very top.For the second year in a row we win the award for the most equipped gym in the city of Belgrade,and this year we became the best gym in our city.

Our team

Our coaches are educated students and professors from the Faculty of Sports and Physical Education, as well as from the Faculty of Medicine. They are aimed at working with recreational athletes,people with high pressure, people with diabetes, people with cardiorespiratory problems, people with spinal and limb deformities,etc. Whatever problem you may have, our coaches are here to help you get to the goal you want.
